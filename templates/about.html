03 : About me

Born and raised in beautiful Quebec City, one of North America’s top tourist destinations, I discovered a passion for luxury hospitality at the age of 15.

Through over a decade in this industry, I have worked as Concierge and Chef Concierge in some of the best properties across the country, was honoured to not only be the youngest member to ever join the closed circle of Les Clefs d’Or at the age of 22 but to have also won the Les Clefs d’Or Awards 2019, allowing me to represent Canada in the international competition in Cannes, France, among over 130 members.

Having studied marketing and visual arts, my creativity and witty, unconventional thinking are valuable assets for the exceptional individuals I assist and to the businesses I provide marketing consulting services to.
